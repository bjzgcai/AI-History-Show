# DENDRAL

## Full Name
Chemical inference paper record

## Year / Period
1965

## Type
Scientific Discovery

## One-Sentence Summary
DENDRAL is an AI100 achievement in Expert systems.

## Hero Description
DENDRAL was one of the first expert systems to show that domain knowledge could make AI practically useful. It helped infer molecular structures from mass-spectrometry evidence by combining search with expert chemical rules. The project shifted attention from general reasoning alone to knowledge-rich systems.

## People & Place
- Edward Feigenbaum: Leader in expert-system AI

Key place: Stanford University

## Historical Background
General-purpose problem solving was not enough for hard scientific tasks. DENDRAL showed that carefully encoded expertise could focus search.

## Canonical Source
Chemical inference paper record. https://doi.org/10.1021/ci60034a002

## Core Idea
The system generated candidate molecular structures and rejected impossible ones using chemical constraints.

## Key Concepts
- Knowledge Engineering: Experts help encode rules that guide the system.
- Generate and Test: Candidate structures are generated, then filtered by constraints.
- Mass Spectrum: Chemical evidence supplies clues about molecular fragments.

## Impact
DENDRAL helped launch the expert-system era and influenced knowledge engineering in medicine, chemistry, and enterprise systems.
