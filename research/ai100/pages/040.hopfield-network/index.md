# Hopfield Network

## Full Name
Neural Networks and Physical Systems with Emergent Collective Computational Abilities

## Year / Period
1982

## Type
Neural Network

## One-Sentence Summary
Hopfield Network is a major AI100 achievement connecting Neural Network to later AI systems.

## Hero Description
Hopfield networks connected neural computation with physics. A pattern could be stored as an attractor in an energy landscape, and a noisy input could settle back into the closest memory. This made associative memory, optimization, and neural dynamics part of the same conceptual frame.

## People & Place
- John J. Hopfield: Inventor of Hopfield networks

Key place: California Institute of Technology

## Historical Background
Before deep learning, researchers were already asking how large sets of simple units could compute collectively. Hopfield gave this question an energy-based formulation.

## Canonical Source
Neural Networks and Physical Systems with Emergent Collective Computational Abilities. John J. Hopfield, Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, 1982. https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.79.8.2554

## Core Idea
The network updates units to reduce an energy function, so stored memories become stable attractors.

## Key Concepts
- Attractor: A stored pattern is a stable state that nearby noisy patterns tend to approach.
- Energy Function: The network changes state in a direction that lowers energy.
- Associative Memory: Partial or corrupted inputs can recover a complete stored pattern.

## Impact
Hopfield’s 2024 Nobel recognition made the connection between statistical physics and machine learning visible far beyond AI history circles.
