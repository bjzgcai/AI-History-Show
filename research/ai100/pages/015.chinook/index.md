# Chinook

## Full Name
Chinook

## Year / Period
1989-2007

## Type
Game AI

## One-Sentence Summary
Chinook is an AI100 achievement in game search and solved games.

## Hero Description
Chinook pushed game AI from competitive play toward mathematical solution. The University of Alberta team combined opening books, deep alpha-beta search, expert evaluation, and enormous endgame databases, eventually proving that perfect play in checkers leads to a draw.

## People & Place
- Jonathan Schaeffer: Leader of the Chinook team

Key place: University of Alberta

## Historical Background
Checkers was complex enough to challenge search algorithms but structured enough for decades of endgame database construction.

## Canonical Source
Checkers Is Solved. https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.1144079

## Core Idea
Chinook paired forward search with solved endgame tables, letting the program connect current choices to proven late-game outcomes.

## Key Concepts
- Opening Book: Human and computer game knowledge narrows early choices.
- Endgame Database: Late positions are precomputed to proven outcomes.
- Weak Solution: The game-theoretic value is known from the starting position.

## Impact
It became a landmark for game-solving research and for measuring how far exhaustive computation and heuristic search can go together.
